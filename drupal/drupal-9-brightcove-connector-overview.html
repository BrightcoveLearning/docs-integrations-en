Introduction

The Drupal-Brightcove connector for Drupal 9 updates the previous version to all you to take advantage of the new features in Drupal 9. While Drupal 9 does not offer significant new features over Drupal 8, it does introduce performance enhancements and update underlying software subsystems to increase security, stability, and maintainability

Installation

To install the connector for Drupal 9, please follow the instructions on the Drupal site.
